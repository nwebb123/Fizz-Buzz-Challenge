FizzBuzz Challenge

The Traditional FizzBuzz Challenge is to write a program that prints the numbers one through one hundred (1-100). But for multiples of three, print "Fizz" instead of the number and for multiples of five print "Buzz". For numbers that are multiples of both three and five, print "FizzBuzz".
